New Menu Tasting Event — You're Invited!

Our team has been huddled in the kitchen (aprons on, taste buds fully engaged) cooking up a brand-new menu that we're honestly a little too excited about. We tested, we tweaked, we may have argued lovingly over how much cheese is "too much cheese" — and now it's finally ready to share with you. Come be our taste-testing partners in crime! Your feedback genuinely shapes what ends up on the menu, so don't be shy.

📅 Date: Saturday, October 7th
🕚 Time: 11:00 AM – 12:00 PM
📍 Location: Café Event Space

Spots are limited, and once we're full, we're full — so reserve early before someone else eats your slice (literally). We can't wait to see you there — and we promise the only thing you'll regret is not trying enough! 🍽️✨
